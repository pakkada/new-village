About Our Village

Green Valley is a picturesque village nestled in the foothills of the Western Ghats. Founded in 1823, it was named for its lush green landscapes that remain vibrant throughout the year.

With a population of approximately 1,200 people, our village is known for its harmonious blend of cultures, with Hindu, Muslim, and Christian communities celebrating festivals together.

The primary occupation is agriculture, with rice, coconut, and spices being our main crops. Traditional arts like pottery and weaving are still practiced by local artisans.
